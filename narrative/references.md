# References

The literature pointers used in the narrative documents.

## Lattices

* E. S. Barnes and G. E. Wall, *Some extreme forms defined in terms of Abelian groups*, J. Australian Math. Soc. **1** (1959), 47–63.
* A. R. Calderbank, P. J. Cameron, W. M. Kantor, J. J. Seidel, *$\mathbb{Z}_4$-Kerdock codes, orthogonal spreads, and extremal Euclidean line-sets*, Proc. London Math. Soc. **75** (1997), 436–480.
* S. Forest, D. Gosset, V. Kliuchnikov, D. McKinnon, *Exact synthesis of single-qubit unitaries over Clifford-cyclotomic gate sets*, J. Math. Phys. **56** (2015), 082201.
* V. Kliuchnikov, S. Schönnenbeck, *Stabilizer operators and Barnes–Wall lattices*, arXiv:2404.17677 (2024). The lattice-automorphism characterization of the Clifford group and the minimal-vector identification (Theorems 3.4 and 4.3) are from this paper.

## Stabilizer formalism and Clifford groups

* P. Selinger, *Generators and relations for *n*-qubit Clifford operators*, Logical Methods in Computer Science **11** (2015), Issue 2:10.
* M. Backens, *The ZX-calculus is complete for stabilizer quantum mechanics*, New J. Phys. **16** (2014), 093021.
* E. Jeandel, S. Perdrix, R. Vilmart, *A complete axiomatisation of the ZX-calculus for Clifford+*T* quantum mechanics*, LICS 2018.
* S. Aaronson, D. Gottesman, *Improved simulation of stabilizer circuits*, Phys. Rev. A **70** (2004), 052328.

## Decoding and bounded-distance algorithms

* D. Micciancio, A. Nicolosi, *Efficient bounded distance decoders for Barnes–Wall lattices*, IEEE Symposium on Information Theory, 2008.

## *T*-count, magic, and certified floors

* M. Amy, D. Maslov, M. Mosca, *Polynomial-time *T*-depth optimization of Clifford+*T* circuits via matroid partitioning*, IEEE Trans. CAD **33** (2014), 1476–1489.
* M. Amy, M. Mosca, **T*-count and *T*-depth of any multi-qubit unitary*, IEEE Trans. Inf. Theory **65** (2019), 2230–2243.
* M. Amy, A. N. Glaudell, N. J. Ross, *Number-theoretic characterizations of some restricted Clifford+*T* circuits*, Quantum **4** (2020), 252.
* M. Beverland, E. Campbell, M. Howard, V. Kliuchnikov, *Lower bounds on the non-Clifford resources for quantum computations*, Quantum Sci. Technol. **5** (2020), 035009.
* J. Jiang, X. Wang, *Lower bound the *T*-count via unitary stabilizer nullity*, Phys. Rev. A **108** (2023), 022609.

## Diagonal hierarchies and Reed–Muller codes

* S. X. Cui, D. Gottesman, A. Krishna, *Diagonal gates in the Clifford hierarchy*, Phys. Rev. A **95** (2017), 012329.
* H. Bombin, M. A. Martin-Delgado, *Topological quantum distillation*, Phys. Rev. Lett. **97** (2006), 180501.

* J. Carette, C. Heunen, R. Kaarsgaard, A. Sabry, *With a few square roots, quantum computing is as easy as $\pi$*, Proc. ACM Program. Lang. **8** (POPL), 2024.
